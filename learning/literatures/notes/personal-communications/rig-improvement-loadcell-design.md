## Unique contribution
Practical engineering design guidance for a laboratory rig used in tribological testing. Specifies the material and geometry relationship for the ring load cell: increasing wall thickness (reducing the internal hole diameter) enables higher load capacity.

## Methods / results / takeaways
- Ring-type load cell designed for up to 1000 kg; deflects ~0.2 mm at that load using EN24 steel (Z condition).
- Load capacity can be increased by thickening the ring wall (reducing the central hole diameter).
- Bolt torque and sizing calculations for the cylinder support are required as prerequisite to rig assembly.
