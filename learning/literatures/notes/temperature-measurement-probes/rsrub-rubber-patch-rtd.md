## Overview
A one-page product datasheet from Sterling Sensors (Oldham, UK) describing the RSRUB series Rubber Patch RTD — a surface-mount Pt100 resistance temperature detector encased in a self-adhesive silicone rubber patch for easy attachment to flat surfaces such as HVAC ducts, pipes, and other equipment.

## Unique contribution
Provides specific dimensional, wiring, and accuracy data for a commercial surface-mount RTD product — notably the silicone rubber enclosure that provides chemical and oil resistance combined with a self-adhesive backing, and the optional over-patch cover (40 mm square) for added insulation and mechanical protection.

## Key concepts
- RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector)
- Pt100 element
- 3-wire configuration
- Class B accuracy
- PFA twisted wire lead
- Surface-mount temperature sensing
- HVAC temperature measurement
- Temperature mapping
- Self-adhesive silicone rubber patch

## Methods / results / takeaways
- Sensor type: Pt100, 3-wire, Class B accuracy
- Operating range: -50 °C to +150 °C
- Patch dimensions: 15 mm × 30 mm; cover: 40 mm × 40 mm
- Standard lead: 7/0.2 mm PFA twisted wire, 2 m or 5 m; 50 mm stripped wire termination
- Resistant to many oils, liquids, and chemicals due to silicone rubber housing
- Suitable for surface measurement, HVAC ducts/pipes, and temperature mapping applications
- Custom lead lengths and connectors available to order
- Manufactured in the UK by Sterling Sensors Ltd
