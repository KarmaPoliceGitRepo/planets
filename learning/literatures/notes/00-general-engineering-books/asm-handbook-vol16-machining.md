## Overview
This is Volume 16 of the ASM Handbook series, a comprehensive engineering reference published by ASM International in 1989, dedicated to machining processes. The ASM Handbook series is a canonical reference for materials science and manufacturing engineering. Volume 16 covers the full range of machining operations including turning, milling, drilling, grinding, and non-traditional processes, as applied to metals and engineered materials.

## Unique contribution
As a standards-level reference, this volume consolidates empirical data, process parameters, tooling specifications, and materials-specific guidance that are not easily found in textbooks. It serves as a practical handbook for machinists, process engineers, and researchers needing data tables and best-practice guidance for machining operations on specific alloy classes.
