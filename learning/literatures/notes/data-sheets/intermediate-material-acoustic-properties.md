## Overview
A spreadsheet database of measured acoustic properties for a wide range of epoxy and polymer materials used as intermediate layers (backing and matching layers) in ultrasonic transducer fabrication. Properties are measured at multiple frequencies (0.5, 1, 2.25, and sometimes 5 MHz) in water using pulse-echo and through-transmission methods.

## Unique contribution
Provides experimentally measured longitudinal and shear wave velocities, densities, acoustic impedances, elastic moduli (Young's, bulk, shear), elastic constants (c11, c44), compliance constants (s11, s44), Poisson's ratio, and longitudinal/shear attenuation for each material. This is a ready-to-use look-up table for transducer designers selecting backing and matching layer materials.

## Key concepts
- Longitudinal wave velocity (Vl)
- Shear wave velocity (Vs)
- Acoustic impedance (Z, in MRayl)
- Elastic stiffness constants (c11, c44)
- Elastic compliance constants (s11, s44)
- Poisson's ratio
- Longitudinal and shear attenuation (dB/m)
- Araldite epoxy systems (MY750, HY1300/CY1301, HY956EN/CY221)
- Stycast epoxy systems (2651-40 mica-filled, 2850-FT alumina-filled)
- Alumina and aluminium nitride fillers
- Silicone rubbers (RTV664, Ward Bekker)
- Flexibilisers (DY040, DY041)
- Aqualene, Devcon liquid steel, Perspex, Aluminium, Brass reference samples

## Methods / results / takeaways
- Hard-set Vantico HY1300/CY1301 shows Vl ≈ 2438–2519 m/s and Z ≈ 2.8–2.9 MRayl at 0.5–3 MHz; adding DY041 flexibiliser (up to 100%) reduces Vl to ~2258 m/s and increases attenuation dramatically.
- Alumina-filled Stycast 2850-FT (3–4% catalyst 9) has Vl ≈ 3340–3480 m/s and Z ≈ 7.6–8.3 MRayl, making it useful for high-impedance matching layers.
- Soft Set (Vantico HY956/CY208) is highly attenuating in shear: >11,000 dB/m at 1.25 MHz.
- RTV664 silicone rubber has very low Vl (~986–1022 m/s) and near-zero shear stiffness — useful as a compliant de-coupling layer.
- Aluminium reference sample: Vl ≈ 6214–6306 m/s; Brass: Vl ≈ 4218–4513 m/s (good for calibration checks).
- Sheet 2 provides detailed frequency sweeps from 0.25 to 3 MHz for Vantico Hard Set and Medium Set, allowing attenuation-frequency curve fitting.
