## Overview
A brief personal notes text file recording a procurement order from PI (Physik Instrumente / PI Ceramic) for piezoelectric discs, specifying material grades and frequencies needed. Not a technical document; records which PI Ceramic material grades (PIC144, PIC155, PIC184) and frequencies (0.2, 0.5, 1.0 MHz) were ordered, and notes about preferred geometries.

## Unique contribution
Provides context for why the PI Ceramic catalog and DEL Piezo datasheet exist in this folder — they support active transducer fabrication work. Notes that PIC144 was selected for longitudinal applications due to its good kp-to-kt ratio. Small batches (5 or 10 pieces) were requested for disc and wraparound geometries.

## Key concepts
- PIC144, PIC155, PIC184 material grades
- Longitudinal disc transducers
- Wraparound piezo elements
- kp to kt ratio

## Methods / results / takeaways
- PIC144 identified as preferred for longitudinal applications (favorable kp/kt).
- Frequencies ordered: 0.2, 0.5, and 1.0 MHz for PIC144 and PIC155; fewer frequencies for PIC184.
- Disc geometry preferred where possible; wraparound geometry as secondary option.
