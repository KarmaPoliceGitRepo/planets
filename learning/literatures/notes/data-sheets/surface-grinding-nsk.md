## Overview
A surface grinding specification sheet from NSK describing two surface grinding service codes (4F2GS and 6GS) for ceramic or steel components. Provides machining ranges, tolerances, degrees of parallelism/flatness/squareness, and surface roughness specifications for the T (thickness), A (length), and B (width) dimensions of ground rectangular specimens.

## Unique contribution
Specifies the achievable tolerances and surface quality metrics for NSK's precision grinding services — directly relevant for sourcing ground ceramic or steel plates for ultrasonic transducer fabrication or precision mechanics. Includes specific flatness (0.008–0.015 mm) and parallelism tolerances as a function of thickness.

## Key concepts
- Surface grinding codes (4F2GS, 6GS)
- Parallelism of T surfaces
- Flatness of T surfaces
- Squareness of reference surface
- Surface roughness (Ra 1.6 µm for T and reference surfaces; Ra 6.3 µm for other surfaces)
- T, A, B dimensional limits

## Methods / results / takeaways
- 4F2GS: T dimension 2.5–900 mm, A up to 4000 mm, B up to 2000 mm; tolerance +0.2 to +0.4 mm on specified dimension.
- 6GS: T dimension up to 300 mm, A up to 400 mm, B up to 400 mm.
- Flatness of T surfaces: 0.008 mm (FGS code), 0.012–0.015 mm (4F2GS/6GS).
- Reference surface roughness: Ra 1.6 µm; T surfaces: Ra 1.6 µm; other surfaces: Ra 6.3 µm.
- Parallelism tolerance varies by T dimension range (5 µm for T<16 mm, up to 15 µm for larger T).
