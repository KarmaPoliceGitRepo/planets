## Overview
A large product catalog from PI Ceramic GmbH covering the full range of their piezoelectric ceramic products including discs, rings, tubes, bending elements, and multilayer actuators. Content includes material data, dimensional catalogs, and application guidance for ultrasonic, actuator, and sensor uses.

## Unique contribution
PI's catalog is notable for its breadth of standard product geometries and the inclusion of their proprietary material grades (PIC144, PIC155, PIC184, PIC255, etc.), which are the reference materials used throughout much of the academic and industrial ultrasonic transducer community.

## Key concepts
- PIC material grades (hard and soft PZT families)
- Piezoelectric discs, rings, tubes, plates
- Multilayer actuator elements
- Resonant frequency and coupling factors per geometry
- Ordering codes and dimensional tolerances

## Methods / results / takeaways
- PIC144 is noted for a good kp-to-kt ratio, making it favorable for longitudinal (disc) applications.
- PIC155 and PIC184 are also listed for longitudinal disc and wraparound transducer configurations.
- Standard stock items are available in small batches (5 or 10 pieces) for prototyping.
- Full material property tables (density, Curie temperature, coupling coefficients, elastic constants, dielectric properties) are given per grade.
