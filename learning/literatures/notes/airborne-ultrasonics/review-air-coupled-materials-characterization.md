## Overview
Comprehensive 2014 review of air-coupled ultrasonics for materials characterization and NDE. Covers transducer developments (capacitive, piezocomposite, 1-3 composite), Lamb wave inspection, elastic stiffness measurement, acoustic tomography, laser ultrasound, paper and wood characterization, civil structures, and nonlinear ultrasonics. Special attention to work by Hosten/Bordeaux group.

## Unique contribution
Synthesises ~30 years of air-coupled NDE development, mapping the field from transducer technology through signal processing to application domains. Identifies Lamb wave + air-coupled technique as the dominant materials-characterization modality, and surveys nonlinear ACU as an emerging direction.

## Methods / results / takeaways
- Review covers: transducers and electronics, Lamb-wave coincidence method, elastic stiffness via transmission, imaging/tomography, arrays, nonlinear methods.
- Lamb wave coincidence: beam incident at θ satisfying c_phase = c_air/sin(θ) generates plate Lamb modes; leaky Lamb wave received by second air-coupled transducer.
- Materials: composites, wood, paper, concrete, ceramics, food products covered.
- Nonlinear ACU: harmonic generation in plates; contact-resonance methods combined with air detection.
