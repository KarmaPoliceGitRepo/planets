# Air-Coupled and Water Immersion Sectorized Array Transducers for Industrial and Medical Endoscopy

- **Source:** Gomez Alvarez-Arenas 2017 sectorized array endoscopy
- **Drive link:** https://drive.google.com/file/d/1JXuMneEQwEB9bFE3xN9pXm2pWkvHvbQa/view
- **Type:** paper
- **Author/Year:** Gómez Alvarez-Arenas & Díez, 2017 (IEEE Ultrasonics Symposium)
- **Coverage:** full

## Overview
Designs 8-element kerfless sectorized array transducers combined with a conical reflector to generate a 2D cylindrical acoustic field for endoscopy — one air-coupled (400 kHz, 25 mm diameter) and two water-coupled (4 MHz × 15 mm and 2 MHz × 5 mm). Demonstrates pipe inner diameter and wall thickness measurement in Al and steel pipes.

## Unique contribution
Introduces sectorized array + conical reflector as a simpler, more alignment-tolerant alternative to mechanically-rotating single-element endoscopes. The 8-element design allows operation when the transducer centre is not precisely on the pipe axis and for non-cylindrical cross-sections.

## Key concepts
- Sectorized transducer array (8 elements, kerfless)
- Conical reflector for 2D cylindrical field
- Ultrasonic endoscopy
- Air-coupled 400 kHz array
- Water-coupled 4 MHz and 2 MHz arrays
- Pipe inner diameter from TOF
- Wall thickness from time-domain separation or spectral resonances
- 1-3 piezocomposite

## Methods / results / takeaways
- Air-coupled array: 400 kHz, 25 mm diam., center freq 0.45 MHz, echo amplitude 45 mV at 200 mm from 20 mm cylinder via conical reflector.
- Directivity characterised by rotating cylindrical reflector at 200 mm in 5° steps.
- Water-coupled 4 MHz: 6-dB BW 40%, peak sensitivity −28 to −32 dB; 2 MHz: BW 26–33%, sensitivity −48 to −54 dB.
- Pipe results: Al 3.5 mm wall → echoes separated 1.1 μs → estimated 3.47 mm (actual 3.5 mm); Steel 2.1 mm wall → overlapped echoes → thickness resonances 2.8 + 4.2 MHz → estimated 2.1 mm.
- Al 1.5 mm wall: thickness resonances ~2.1–2.2 MHz → estimated correctly.
