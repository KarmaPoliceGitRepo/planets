## Overview
First of Auld's two-volume treatise on acoustic fields and waves in solids. Volume I covers the fundamental theory of elastic waves in anisotropic and piezoelectric media: linear acoustics, tensor notation, constitutive equations, energy theorems, acoustic plane waves in isotropic solids (including the transmission-line analogy), surface acoustic waves, and the foundations of piezoelectric transducer theory. A standard graduate reference in the field.
