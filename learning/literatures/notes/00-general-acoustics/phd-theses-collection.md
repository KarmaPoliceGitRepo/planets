## Overview
A collection of PhD theses (and one USAF technical report) spanning ultrasonics in manufacturing, nonlinear wave generation, acoustoelasticity, Rayleigh wave tribology, and acoustic metamaterials. Key research themes include: ultrasonic measurement in industrial processes (rolling, machining), nonlinear acoustics for NDE, residual stress/fatigue sensing via acoustoelastic effect, surface wave interactions with engineering interfaces, and metamaterial-based acoustic manipulation.
