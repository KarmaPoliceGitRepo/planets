## Overview
Individual article PDFs from the journal "Sensors and Actuators" Volume 2 (1981–82), one of the earliest dedicated sensor/transducer journals. Articles cover solid-state sensors (semiconductor gas sensors, ISFET, MOS structures, capacitive pressure, Hall effect, piezoelectric), materials (PVF2 transducers, thick-film resistors), signal conversion/processing, and special topics (ultrasonic proximity sensor, microdielectrometry, VLSI integration). This collection documents the state of sensor technology at the dawn of the MEMS era.

## Selected papers of acoustic/ultrasonic relevance
- **An Ultrasonic Proximity Sensor Operating in Air** (1981) — air-coupled ultrasonic ranging sensor design
- **Cylindrical PVF2 Electromechanical Transducers** (1981) — PVDF polymer acoustic transducer
- **A Coated Piezoelectric Crystal to Detect Organophosphorus Compounds** (1981) — quartz crystal microbalance (QCM) chemical sensor
