## Overview
Second volume of Auld's treatise. Covers advanced topics: acoustic waveguides, resonators, microwave acoustic devices (bulk acoustic wave filters, SAW devices), scattering theory, and transducer coupling networks. Contains the definitive treatment of the KLM transducer model and microwave acoustic engineering design methods.
