## Overview
A mixed collection of journal papers spanning general acoustics measurement, NDT applications, and specific techniques. Key themes include: acoustic intensity measurement (Jacobsen 1991), Kramers-Kronig relations for attenuation/velocity dispersion (Mangulis 1964), wave propagation in anisotropic media (Crampin 1984), ultrasonic velocity and attenuation measurement methods, coded excitation pulse compression, Time-of-Flight measurement via DSP, high-frequency pulse-echo at 200 MHz (Khuri-Yakub 1977), viscosity measurement by torsional waves (Bradfield 1956), bulk viscosity by acoustics (Dukhin 2009), and tribological applications (Dwyer-Joyce 2005).
