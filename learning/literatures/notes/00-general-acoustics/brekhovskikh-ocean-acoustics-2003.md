## Overview
Third and final edition of Brekhovskikh and Lysanov's ocean acoustics textbook (first published 1982 in Russian; English 1982; 2nd ed. 1991). Compared to the 1982 edition (also in this folder), the 2003 edition incorporates acoustic tomography, ambient noise modelling advances, and updated numerical methods. See note `brekhovskikh-ocean-acoustics-1982.md` for full content analysis of the 1982 edition.
