## Overview
A rigorous mathematical treatment of elastic wave propagation, covering fundamental theory through to advanced topics like diffraction, scattering, and dynamic crack problems. A standard graduate-level reference in solid mechanics and seismology. Achenbach's systematic development of the reciprocal theorem and its applications is widely cited.
