## Overview
Product datasheet for O'Keefe Controls air proximity sensors (jet-type pneumatic proximity switches) and associated signal conditioning components (air/air amplifiers and air/electric switches). Covers operating specifications, part numbers, and performance parameters.

## Unique contribution
Provides concrete application specifications for commercial pneumatic proximity sensors: supply pressure, sensing range, response time, body material, and part numbers for a complete sensing system including downstream amplifiers and electric output switches.

## Key concepts
- Pneumatic proximity sensor

- Air/electric switch
- Supply pressure
- Sensing range
- Response time

## Methods / results / takeaways
- **Air proximity sensor**: supply pressure up to 10 psig; air flow ~0.5 scfm @ 1 psig; sensing range up to 1/8"; response time 1 ms; stainless steel body.
- Part numbers: OKC-818, OKC-424.
- **Air/air amplifier (KLUF2)**: switches at 2" H₂O differential; supply up to 100 psig.
- **Air/electric switches**: OKC-661 (200 mA, 0.5" H₂O min actuation), OKC-664 (10 A), OKC-819 (60 mA, 10 ms response).
- System suitable for presence/proximity detection in industrial automation.
