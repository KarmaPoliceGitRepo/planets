## Overview
The ASM International "Atlas of Stress-Strain Curves" is the standard reference compilation of stress-strain curves for engineering alloys. The 2nd edition contains over 1,400 stress-strain curves for a broad range of commercial metals and alloys including steels, aluminium alloys, titanium alloys, superalloys, cast irons, and copper alloys. Data are presented at various temperatures, strain rates, and heat treatment conditions.

## Unique contribution
Provides the largest publicly available compilation of engineering and true stress-strain curves in a single reference, covering materials at ambient and elevated temperatures. Each curve typically records yield strength, UTS, elongation, and reduction in area alongside the graphical curve — enabling direct lookup for design and simulation input.
