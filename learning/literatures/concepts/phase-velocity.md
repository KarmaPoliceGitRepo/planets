# Phase Velocity
**Aliases:** c = ω/k; vp; wave velocity; sound velocity; speed of sound; acoustic velocity; velocity of sound; phase speed

**Definition:** Phase velocity is the speed at which a constant-phase surface (wavefront) advances: v_p = ω/k = fλ. For non-dispersive bulk waves in homogeneous isotropic media it is a material constant (e.g., V_L or V_S); in dispersive guided waves, multilayered media, or scattering materials, v_p is frequency-dependent. Phase velocity is the primary observable in pulse-overlap, sing-around, and phase-comparison ultrasonic velocity measurements, and it links directly to elastic moduli and material density through the wave equation.
