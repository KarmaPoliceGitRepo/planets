# Digital Signal Processing (Overview)
**Aliases:** DSP; digital signal processing; signal processing; signal analysis; discrete-time systems; LTI systems

**Definition:** Digital signal processing (DSP) encompasses the theory and algorithms for representing, transforming, and extracting information from discrete-time digital signals. Core topics include: the Fourier / Z / Laplace transforms and their relationships; digital filter design (FIR, IIR); spectral analysis via FFT and PSD estimation; sampling and aliasing; multirate processing; statistical signal processing (noise, SNR, Kalman filter, GPR); adaptive and sparse methods (wavelet shrinkage, compressive sensing). In ultrasonic measurement the main DSP tasks are time-of-flight estimation, pulse deconvolution, temperature compensation, and defect characterisation.
