# Tribology
**Aliases:** tribological system; tribosystem; friction-wear-lubrication science

**Definition:** Tribology is the science and engineering of interacting surfaces in relative motion, encompassing friction, wear, and lubrication. A tribosystem is defined by the four components of body, counterbody, interfacial medium, and environment; changes to any element alter the tribological response. The discipline spans scales from molecular films to macroscopic contact mechanics and is central to engine reliability, manufacturing process design, and material selection.
