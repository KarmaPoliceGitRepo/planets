# Process Capability
**Aliases:** Cp; Cpk; process capability index; process capability ratio; capability study; statistical process control; SPC; control chart; 6-sigma; tolerance; variation; bore diameter tolerance

**Definition:** Process capability indices quantify how well a manufacturing process meets specification limits relative to its natural variation. Cp = (USL − LSL)/(6σ) measures potential capability; Cpk = min[(USL − x̄)/3σ, (x̄ − LSL)/3σ] accounts for centering. Cpk = 1.33 (4-sigma) has been the traditional automotive bore-honing target; modern engine bore specifications now require Cpk ≥ 2.0 (6-sigma). A Cpk < 1 means the process is not capable of meeting specification. Process capability is a key output metric of honing process qualification alongside surface texture parameters.
